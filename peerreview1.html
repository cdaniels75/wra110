Instructions

In your D2L discussion areas for the Lived Experiences Drafting Activity, respond each of your groupmate's drafts. In your response, evaluate the completeness and effectiveness with respect to the assignment's requirements (i.e. are things missing? are irrelevant things present? does the draft seem to fit the assignment?) Additionally, provide a detailed revision plan to help guide each of your groupmates as they revise. NB: Your responses will be graded for their effort and their usefulness in the revision process
